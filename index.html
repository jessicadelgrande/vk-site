What we provide

+ Lunch

1 in 8 households is food insecure.

Students can't learn on an empty stomach, which is why we provide them with complimentary lunch.

+ Tools

1 in 4 parents can't afford school supplies.

Not everyone can afford the latest school supplies or a laptop, which is why we provide our students with free tools and resources.

+ Role Models

70% of our team are women or POC.

Diversity doesn't happen by accident. Our team and our mentors reflect the students and communities we serve.

Meet our partners

The work we do would not be possible without the generous support of our partners, who are helping us raise a new generation of entrepreneurs.

+ Lighthouse Labs

Lighthouse Labs is Canada's premier coding bootcamp for web and mobile software development and available in multiple cities across Canada.

+ NodeDay Toronto

NodeDay Toronto is a one-day Node.js conference by enterprise leaders, for enterprise leaders. The conference engages C-Level attendees and industry leaders for a day of talks, panels, workshops and round-table discussion.

+ Enactus

Enactus Ryerson is part of a global network of hubs in 36 countries around the world. The organization helps post-secondary students who see business as a way to address social issues.

+ CommuniHelp

CommuniHelp is a skill-based matching platform that matches professionals to volunteer opportunities with social good organizations.

+ TCHC

The Toronto Community Housing Corporation (TCHC) is a public housing agency in Toronto, Ontario, Canada and the second-largest housing provider in North America.
